Frequently Asked Questions About Addiction Treatment in Ebbagarh

What types of addiction do you treat? +
We treat drug addiction (opioids, cannabis, cocaine, synthetic drugs), alcohol addiction, tobacco addiction, behavioral addictions (gambling), and co-occurring mental health disorders. Each condition requires a customized treatment approach.
How long does treatment typically last? +
Treatment duration varies based on individual needs, typically 28-90 days for residential programs and 3-12 months for outpatient treatment. We customize the timeline to ensure proper recovery without rush.
Is treatment confidential? +
Yes, 100% confidential. We follow strict privacy protocols compliant with all regulations. Your recovery journey is personal, and we respect your privacy completely.
Can I visit the facility before admission? +
Yes, we welcome facility visits. You can tour the center, meet our team, and understand our programs firsthand. Call +91 9755209205 to schedule a visit.
What is medical detoxification? +
Medical detoxification is a safe, supervised process to remove substances from your body while managing withdrawal symptoms with medication and medical care. Our doctors ensure maximum comfort and safety.
How involved is family in the treatment? +
Family involvement is crucial. We offer family counseling, education, and support programs. Family members learn to understand addiction, set boundaries, and support recovery effectively.
What happens after completing the program? +
Aftercare is critical. We provide ongoing counseling, alumni support groups, relapse prevention strategies, and community resources to ensure long-term sobriety.
How much does treatment cost? +
Cost varies based on program type and duration. We offer flexible payment options and transparent pricing. Call +91 9755209205 for a detailed cost discussion suited to your needs.
Can I get treatment while working? +
Yes. Our outpatient programs are designed for working professionals. Sessions are scheduled at convenient times, allowing you to maintain your job while receiving quality treatment.
How do I know if someone has an addiction problem? +
Signs include withdrawal from normal activities, behavioral changes, health problems, financial issues, legal troubles, and relationship strain. If you're concerned about someone, contact us for guidance.
